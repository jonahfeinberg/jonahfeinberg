About the Shop

Something Special has been part of Lincoln for over 30 years. It started as a small passion project and somehow never stopped growing. Every item on the shelves was chosen because someone on our team genuinely loved it — not because it fit a trend.

We carry jewelry, home goods, cards, and gifts for pretty much every occasion. If you can't find what you're looking for, just ask. We've gotten pretty good at tracking things down.
