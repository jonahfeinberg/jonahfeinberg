Scratch Intros

Before I really knew anything that I would like to do, I started out on Scratch. While there, I discovered motion design, or "intros" as they're called on the platform, and I made a bunch of them for fun. These were short clips that I created using Scratch's block-based coding system, set to music to introduce a creator.
