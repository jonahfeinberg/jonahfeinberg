The Backstory

Subject Colors began as a simple argument in a classroom — one that escalated far beyond its original purpose. What started as a debate over whether math is blue or red eventually grew into a full-scale mission to bring order, clarity, and ultimate truth to the universe and conquer darkness. After countless polls, harsh and bloody wars, and a near-universe-ending bomb, this site was created to settle the matter once and for all.

Our goal is simple: to eliminate chaos in your backpack, unify binder colors across the globe, and put an end to the suffering caused by people who think science is blue.
