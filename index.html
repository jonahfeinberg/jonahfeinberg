A little about me

I'm Jonah Feinberg — a sophomore at Lincoln-Sudbury Regional High School, class of 2028. I spend most of my time building things with code, shooting photos, or on the volleyball court. When I'm not doing any of that, I'm probably gaming, editing video, or out on a walk with my dog Jasper.

I'm drawn to subjects that sit at the intersection of people and systems — psychology, sociology, computer science. I find it fascinating how technology shapes behavior and how understanding people can make you a better builder. I'm still figuring out exactly where I want to take all of that, but I'm in no rush.

Jasper is a rescue from Antigua — an American Village Dog with a lot of personality and zero chill. He's probably the most photographed subject in my camera roll.
